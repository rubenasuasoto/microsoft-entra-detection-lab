# Tuning guide

Tuning should be evidence-based and owned by the environment.

Acceptable tuning context:

- Approved source ownership.
- Change ticket or access review reference.
- Named service or automation owner.
- Time-bounded exception.
- Reason the scenario is expected.
- Expiration or review date.

Do not tune away alerts solely because they are noisy. Keep tuning cases visible until the exception is documented and reviewable.
